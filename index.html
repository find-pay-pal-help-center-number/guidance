Secure Connectivity

End-to-end encryption is an important element in keeping your data safe. Some of the methods we use to protect Pay Pal transactions include TLS 1.2-only connections for logins and Certificate Pinning on web and mobile apps to ensure that your TLS connection is established to an authorized Pay Pal server.
